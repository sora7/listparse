		<div class="g_bubble desc">
<b>Eight Bit</b> was originally <a href="http://anidb.net/cr1043">Satelight</a>'s first studio and was involved in the production of several works including Noein, Sousei no Aquarion and Macross F. It became independent in September 2008 under producer <a href="http://anidb.net/cr18711">Kasai Tsutomu</a>. Its headquarters is located in Suginami, Tokyo, Japan. The major line of work is planning and production of animation programs and 3DCG work.<br><br>The studio's first major production was the <a href="http://anidb.net/a6655">Gekijouban Macross F: Itsuwari no Utahime</a> feature film in collaboration with the former parent company Satelight in 2009. The first major work on its own was the television anime series <a href="http://anidb.net/a7727">IS: Infinite Stratos</a> in 2011.
