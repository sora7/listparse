Hirohashi Ryou, real name: Hirohashi Ryou (廣橋涼, different Kanji), was born August 5, 1977. She is a voice actress represented by Aoni Production talent agency. She originates from Nagaoka, Niigata, Japan and is a graduate of Ryukoku University. She is well known for being the voice actor for Miles "Tails" Prower.
