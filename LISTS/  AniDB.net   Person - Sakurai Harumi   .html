Sakurai Harumi, born October 21, 1982, is a voice actress (seiyuu) from Tokyo, Japan currently affiliated with Office Watanabe, but previously was a member of Office CHK and Genki Project. She has been in this business since 2002. When voicing adult games, she is known by other names which include Kazane (風音), Mirau and Izumi Mayu (和泉真優).
