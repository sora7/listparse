Silver Link is an animation production studio. Its headquarters is in Minako, Tokyo. It was founded by Frontline producer Kaneko Hayato, who is also its current representative director.

At first it only acted as production cooperation and subcontracting studio after becoming independent. Its first own production was the TV series Tayutama.
