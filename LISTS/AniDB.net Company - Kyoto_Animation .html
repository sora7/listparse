Kyoto Animation (often shortened to simply KyoAni) is a Japanese animation studio located in Uji, Kyoto Prefecture, Japan. It was established in 1981 by Hatta Youko, became a limited company in 1985 and then became a corporation in 1999. Presided by Hatta Hideaki (Hatta Youko's husband), the company is affiliated with noted studio Sunrise and is also parent to the studio Animation Do.

They have a digital room: Kyoto Animation Digital Eizou Kaihatsushitsu
