<b>Hino Satoshi</b> (born 4 August 1978 in San Francisco, California), is a seiyuu. He can speak English and is skilled in kendo. He has collaborated with <a href="http://anidb.net/cr3">Kugimiya Rie</a> in some anime as protagonists, notably in <a href="http://anidb.net/a3408">Shakugan no Shana</a>, <a href="http://anidb.net/a4146">Zero no Tsukaima</a> and <a href="http://anidb.net/a5630">Nabari no Ou</a>.
