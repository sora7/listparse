Kitamura Eri is a Japanese voice actress (seiyuu) and songstress, who was born on August 16, 1987 in Tokyo, Japan. She is primarily known for her role in Blood+ as Otonashi Saya and singing Tsuyogari, the third ending, for the anime series Konjiki No Gash Bell!!. She is also known to be an amateur manga artist in Japan in her spare time. Kitamura is affiliated with talent agency Early Wing and was formerly affiliated with Kaleidoscope.
