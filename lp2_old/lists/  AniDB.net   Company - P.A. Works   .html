P.A. Works Corporation (short for Progressive Animation Works) is a Japanese animation studio established on 10.11.2000 and located in Nanto, Toyama, Japan. The company's president and founder Horikawa Kenji once worked for Tatsunoko Production, Production I.G, and co-founded Bee Train with his friend Mashimo Kouichi, before forming 越中動画本舗 in 2000. The studio was renamed to P.A. Works in 2002.

The main office is located in Toyama, Japan, which is where the drawing and digital photography take place, and production and direction takes place in their Tokyo office. The company is also involved with animation in video games, along with collaborating in the past with Production I.G and Bee Train on anime productions.
