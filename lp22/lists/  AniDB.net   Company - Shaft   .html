Shaft was founded on September 1, 1975 by Hiroshi Wakao, former Mushi Production's animator. The company was originally founded to paint the animation but later worked on animation production as a subcontractor. They were able to produce their own first original series, Yume kara samenai in 1987.

In 2000, after Shaft cooperating with Gainax to produce a DVD special of remastered Gunbuster, they were in joint production with Gainax to produce Mahoromatic, This Ugly and Beautiful World, and He is My Master. After Hiroshi Wakao's retirement in 2004, Kubota Mitsutoshi has become Shaft's representative director. Their painting group has also been downsized in order to strengthen their digital group.

Since 2004, Shinbou Akiyuki, Oonuma Shin and Oota Kazuhiro have been involved with almost all of Shaft's productions starting with Tsukuyomi: Moon Phase. They heavily inflicted Shinbou's style on each production. Another with fans famous trait of their productions is their continuous use of gags and references to other anime and pop stuff, particularly by using chalkboards.
